        3.5. Old AgeAll stars eventually run out of hydrogen as fuel for the nuclear reaction, as it has been converted to helium

    • Cosmic Redshift
      • The Doppler Effect
        • A moving object will cause the waves to change in wavelength and frequency:
          WavelengthFrequency
          Waves in Front of the SourceDecreaseIncrease
          Waves Behind the SourceIncreaseDecrease
      • Redshift is the increase in the observed wavelength of electromagnetic radiation emitted from receding stars and galaxies
        • Light emitted from distant galaxies appears redshifted in comparison with light emitted on Earth (further away = more redshift)
        • Redshift in the light from distant galaxies is evidence that the Universe is expanding and supports the Big Bang Theory
    • Cosmic Microwave Background Radiation
      • CMBR is the microwave radiation of a specific frequency that is observed at all points in space around us
        • CMBR was produced by the thermal radiation of the hot and dense environment of the early Universe and that this radiation has been expanded into microwave wavelengths as the Universe expanded
